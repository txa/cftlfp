Category Theory

This is an introductory taster course introducing some basic concepts
of caetgory theory relevant in Computer Science. The course
essentially depends on the participants doing exercises and attending
exercise sessions. The following topics are covered to some degree:
- Categories
- Functors and natural transformations
- Adjunctions
- Products and coproducts
- Exponentials
- Limits and colimits
- Initial algebras and terminal coalgebras
- Monads and comonads
